About YouthInU

  Founded in Dec 2020, by a first-time women entrepreneur, YouthInU is a destination for young aspirants dreaming to achieve big in life. Our mission is to create a respected conglomerate where our team works and strives hard to delight our customers.

About YouthInU Inc.

  Youthinu.com is a website that enables young college kids to find their potential employers during their college days. This is a developed by college kids for college kids. Our goal is help the college kids by providing a means to earn a livelihood during school days. This minimizes the burden of tuition fees and avoid school dropouts and thereby boost the economy. Our motto is simple, Login to our website, create a profile and let the employers chase the talents.
